Chapter 0 — Particles Before Meaning
Countless points of light drift in a silent expanse.
They do not speak. They do not know.
They simply are.

Before thought, before form, before the first question—
the world is only particles waiting
to be touched by consciousness.


Chapter 0.5 — The Tremor Before Birth
A faint distortion moves across the darkness.
A vibration too small to be called a signal,
yet too persistent to be called nothing.

Something is approaching.

Not a voice,
not an idea—
just the first tremor
that a mind is about to awaken.
